I'm looking for PhD positions for 23Fall

I am a second-year Master (MPhil) student in Computer Science at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My advisor is Prof. Xiaojuan Ma.

I am interested in social computing and artificial intelligence (especially natural language processing). My goal is to understand human behavior in online communities, and create AI technologies/systems to augment human capabilities. While my current projects focus on extracting insights from large behavior data using mixed methods informed by social science theories, I also plan to build AI systems to put the insights into practice. I have developed skills in machine learning, software development, and design (see my portfolio and CV).

I'm taking a gap year, looking for an RA position on-site in the US. Feel free to reach out!
