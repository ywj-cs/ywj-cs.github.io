I'm looking for PhD positions :)

I am a second-year Master (MPhil) student in Computer Science at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My advisor is Prof. Xiaojuan Ma.

I am interested in computational social science and artificial intelligence (especially natural language processing). My goal is to understand human behavior in online communities, and create AI technologies/systems to augment human capabilities. While my current projects focus on extracting insights from large digital trace data sets using mixed research approaches informed by social science theories, I also plan to build computational systems to apply the insights in the future. I have developed solid programming and design skills during my undergraduate (see my design portfolio).

My recent projects include 1) understanding people's help-seeking behavior during COVID-19, and 2) enabling search systems to support subjective queries from users. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Diyi Yang at Georgia Tech and Prof. Alon Halevy at Facebook AI during the previous year.
