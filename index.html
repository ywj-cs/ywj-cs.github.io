I'm a Large Language Model researcher and algorithm engineer at Tencent Hunyuan. My current work focuses on improving the performance of foundational models and developing AI applications with them. Previously, I worked in Baidu's Ernie Bot team from 2023 to 2024.

I specialize in developing advanced computational methods to extract actionable insights from complex and large-scale data. I have extensive experience in analyzing massive data, designing studies, and executing projects collaboratively, including tasks like data collection, processing, modeling, visualization, and communicating findings with stakeholders. Trained in cross-disciplines, I was concentrating on addressing critical challenges in various domains such as health, social media, and society. These works have been published in top-tier computer science venues such as CHI, ICWSM, CSCW, and DIS.

I was also a visiting researcher at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Prof. Robert Kraut, Haiyi Zhu, and Diyi Yang. I completed my Master's degree (MPhil) in Computer Science Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I was advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma.

Please feel free to email me if you'd like to chat!
