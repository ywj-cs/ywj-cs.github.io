I am a research associate at Human-Computer Interaction Institute (HCII), Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Prof. Robert Kraut, Haiyi Zhu, and Diyi Yang. I recently completed my Master's degree (MPhil) in Computer Science Engineering (CSE) at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I was advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma.

I am interested in social computing and artificial intelligence (especially natural language processing). My recent research goal is to understand human behavior in online communities, and create intelligent technologies to augment human capabilities. While my current projects focus on extracting insights from large behavior data using mixed methods informed by social science theories, I also plan to build AI systems to put the insights into practice.
